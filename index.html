Here I will talk about the different types of website and what they are used for as well as how this type of website could benefit your online brand!


1. Static Website
Need a simple yet effective online presence? Look no further than a static website. Perfect for showcasing basic information about your business or organization, these sites are quick to set up, provided the content is readily available.

A static website typically includes essential details such as your brand and business information. Prices for a static website range from £200 to £500, depending on your specific needs, and all sites come with standard SEO features.

Depending on content readiness, I can usually have your new site live within 7 days, though this may vary depending on my schedule. Let's discuss your requirements and get your online presence up and running swiftly.



2. Dynamic Website

Looking for a website that offers more than just static content? A dynamic website is the solution. With dynamic functionality, these sites can interact with users, handle data input, and offer personalized experiences.

A dynamic website might include features like user authentication, e-commerce capabilities, or content management systems (CMS) for easy updates. Prices for dynamic websites vary based on complexity, starting from £350 and upwards.

Every dynamic website I create includes essential SEO features to maximize your online visibility. Depending on project scope and content readiness, I aim to have your dynamic site live within a reasonable timeframe, typically within 2-4 weeks, subject to scheduling constraints. Let's collaborate to bring your dynamic web vision to life.



3. E-Commerce Website

Ready to take your business online and start selling products or services? An e-commerce website is the perfect platform. With robust features for online transactions and inventory management, you can reach customers worldwide.

An e-commerce website includes essential elements such as product listings, shopping cart functionality, secure payment gateways, and order management systems. Prices for e-commerce websites vary based on features and customization, typically starting from £1000 and upwards.

Every e-commerce website I develop is optimized for search engines to ensure maximum visibility and reach. Depending on project complexity and content readiness, I aim to have your e-commerce site operational within 4-6 weeks, though this may vary based on workload. Let's work together to build your online store and expand your business reach.



4. Progressive Web App (PWA)

Looking for a modern and engaging way to reach your audience? A Progressive Web App (PWA) offers the best of both worlds: the reach of the web and the functionality of a native app. PWAs deliver a seamless user experience, with features like offline access, push notifications, and fast loading times. They work across various devices and platforms, providing users with a consistent experience.

A PWA typically includes features such as offline caching, responsive design, and app-like interactions. Prices for PWAs vary based on complexity, starting from £1500 and upwards.

Every PWA I develop prioritizes performance and user experience, ensuring that your audience stays engaged. Depending on project requirements and content readiness, I aim to have your PWA live within 6-8 weeks, though timelines may vary based on project scope.

Let's collaborate to bring your vision to life and create a cutting-edge PWA that captivates your audience.

Search Engine Optimisation: is not just about increasing traffic to a website; it's about enhancing the overall online visibility and accessibility of your brand. By integrating SEO into every website I develop, I provide my clients with a comprehensive strategy aimed at maximizing their digital presence and driving organic traffic.

Here's how adding SEO to a website benefits my customers:

  1. Improved Visibility: SEO techniques ensure that your website ranks higher in search engine results pages (SERPs), making it more visible to potential customers. This increased visibility increases the chances of attracting relevant traffic to your site.

  2. Targeted Traffic: SEO strategies are designed to attract users who are actively searching for products or services related to your business. By optimizing your website for specific keywords and phrases, we target the right audience, increasing the likelihood of conversions.

  3. Enhanced User Experience: SEO is not just about keyword optimization; it also involves improving the overall user experience of your website. This includes factors such as site speed, mobile responsiveness, and easy navigation, all of which contribute to higher user satisfaction and engagement.

  4. Long-Term Results: Unlike paid advertising, which stops generating traffic once the budget runs out, SEO provides sustainable, long-term results. By continuously optimizing and refining your website, we ensure that it maintains its visibility and relevance over time.

  5. Competitive Advantage: In today's digital landscape, SEO is essential for staying ahead of the competition. By investing in SEO, you position your business as a credible and authoritative source within your industry, making it more likely to attract customers over competitors.
